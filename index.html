Hello. I’m Khushali, a multimedia journalist, filmmaker, and researcher interested in how people experience systems, cities, and power — and how storytelling can bring data to life.

When I trained as an urban designer in India at CEPT University, learning how cities are planned on paper, I kept noticing what didn’t fit on the page: the messiness of daily life, the politics that operated at different scales, and the emotions and aspirations that make a city what it is.

Studying International Reporting and Documentary Filmmaking at the Newmark Graduate School of Journalism (CUNY), I learned to follow stories in the space between policy, data, and lived reality, and to use different media to tell them.

I’m always drawn to stories where systems meet lived experience.
