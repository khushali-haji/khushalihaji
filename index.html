Hello. I’m Khushali.

I’m a multimedia journalist, filmmaker, and researcher interested in how people experience systems, cities, and power — and how storytelling can bring data to life.

When I trained as an urban designer in India at CEPT University, I kept noticing what didn’t fit on the page: the messiness of daily life, the politics, and the emotions that shape cities.

Storytelling eventually pulled me into journalism and filmmaking. I’ve reported on asylum policies, carbon credit markets, domestic violence, and interfaith communities, and made films about connection, ambition, and belonging.

My work has been recognized by the Silurians Press Club, and my short documentary received a Special Jury Mention at the 2025 Woodstock Film Festival.
