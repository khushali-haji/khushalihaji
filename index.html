Researcher. Designer. Storyteller.

I am a researcher by heart, and a designer by consequence of finding ways to fill those gaps. I'm now applying that instinct to AI-native tools, building products that make complex systems legible, most recently for communities underserved by existing civic and public-interest tech.

My work has taken me from informal settlements in India to forests marked for carbon credits in Liberia, from policy reporting in New York to building AI products for international conflict researchers. I trained as an urban designer, retrained as a journalist, and now design and prototype AI-native tools as the next iteration of that same instinct.

I'm based in New York, looking for roles at the intersection of AI product design, UX research, and public-interest storytelling.
