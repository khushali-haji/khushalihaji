Researcher. Designer. Storyteller.

I am a researcher by heart, and a designer by consequence of finding ways to fill those gaps. I make complexity legible through field investigation, spatial systems, and narrative that doesn't flatten what it's trying to explain.

My work has taken me from informal settlements in India to forests marked for carbon credits in Liberia, from policy reporting in New York to mapping international conflict data on a live map. I trained as an urban designer, retrained as a journalist, and am now building AI-native tools for communities that don't often see themselves in civic tech.

I'm based in New York, looking for roles at the intersection of content design, UX research, and public-interest storytelling.
